HORSE ACCESSORIES GUIDE

Purpose of Common Horse Accessories

Horse riding and care involve a variety of important accessories. Each serves a unique purpose to ensure the safety, comfort, and control of both horse and rider. Here are a few essentials:

Saddle

Provides the rider a secure and comfortable seat while protecting the horse’s back from direct pressure.

Bit & Bridle

Used for communication and control. The bit rests in the horse’s mouth, while the bridle holds it in place and connects to the reins.

Horse Shoes

Protect the horse’s hooves from wear and tear, especially on hard or rough ground.

Girth & Stirrup

The girth secures the saddle to the horse, while stirrups give the rider balance and support.
